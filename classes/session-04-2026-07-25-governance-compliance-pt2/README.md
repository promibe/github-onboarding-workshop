## About This Session

Part 2 goes deeper into protecting sensitive data and managing the risks that come from inside an organisation. You will look at how to classify data (labelling it as Confidential, Public, etc.), apply Data Loss Prevention (DLP) policies that stop sensitive data from being emailed or shared outside the company, and use Microsoft Purview Insider Risk Management to detect unusual behaviour by employees.

**By the end of this session you will be able to:**
- Explain data classification and why labelling matters for compliance
- Configure DLP policies to prevent data leaks
- Describe what insider risk is and how Microsoft Purview helps detect it
- Connect governance concepts to real SC-500 exam scenarios
